How It Works

From Sign-Up to Recovery, in 4 Steps

No waiting rooms. No guessing which YouTube stretch is safe. A plan made for your body, supervised by a clinician who knows your file.

STEP 1

Online Assessment

Complete a virtual assessment with a registered physiotherapist — your history, your pain, your goals.

STEP 2

Get Your Program

Receive a personalized, video-guided exercise plan you can follow at home, on your schedule.

STEP 3

Train & Track

Work through your sessions and log progress. Your physio reviews it and adjusts your plan as your body responds.

STEP 4

Real Help, One Tap Away

Stuck, sore, or unsure? Message your physio, book a virtual check-in, or come see us in-clinic at Nolan Ridge.
