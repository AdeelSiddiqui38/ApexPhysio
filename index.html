Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical treatment take? +

Initial assessments typically take 60 minutes, followed by ongoing sessions of 45-60 minutes. Treatment duration depends on your condition and recovery goals. Most patients see significant improvements within 4-8 weeks.

Do you accept insurance? +

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. We also offer direct billing to insurance providers. Contact us for specific insurance verification and coverage details.

Are your therapists certified? +

All our practitioners are licensed, certified, and regulated by their respective healthcare boards. We maintain the highest standards of professional development and continuing education.

Can I book online? +

Yes! Use our online booking system to schedule appointments at your convenience. You can also call us directly or book via email. Most appointments can be scheduled within 48 hours.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and direct insurance billing. Flexible payment plans are also available for treatment packages.
