4 · Review Engine

Reviews are the #1 local ranking factor and the #1 trust factor for new patients. Process: at discharge or after a visible win, the clinician asks personally; Jane App then sends an SMS with the direct Google review link the same evening. Target: every clinician asks twice a day. Respond to every review within 24h, by name, no medical details.

5 · Google Business Profile & Local SEO Checklist

Google Business Profile

  • Claim & verify listing for "Apex Physio & Wellness Clinic" at Unit 150, 246 Nolanridge Crescent NW
  • Primary category: Physical Therapist · secondary: Massage Therapist, Chiropractor, Sports Medicine Clinic
  • Service area: list all 8 NW communities · hours incl. holiday hours · booking link to Jane App
  • Upload 20+ photos (exterior with signage, treatment rooms, team) + reshoot quarterly
  • List every service with description & price range · seed 8–10 Q&As (parking, direct billing, referrals needed?)
  • Post weekly (offers, tips, team) — GBP posts expire after 7 days

Local SEO

  • NAP (name/address/phone) identical everywhere: site footer, GBP, all directories
  • Citations: Yelp, YellowPages.ca, 411.ca, Bing Places, Apple Maps, RateMDs, pt Health directories
  • LocalBusiness + Physiotherapy schema markup on the site (address, geo, hours, sameAs)
  • One page per service ("Physiotherapy in NW Calgary") + one per community ("Physio near Sage Hill") when the site moves to WordPress
  • Google Search Console + GA4 connected; track booking-section conversions
  • Backlinks: community association sites, sponsored team pages, Calgary physio directories, chamber of commerce

6 · Measurement

Weekly 30-minute review: booked visits by source (ask every new patient + Jane App intake field "How did you hear about us?"), Google Ads cost-per-booking, GBP calls/direction-requests, review count, follower growth. One sheet, five numbers — if a channel can't prove bookings in 60 days, its budget moves.
